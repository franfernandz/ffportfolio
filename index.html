About Me

I design and build digital products from early definition to production.

My background combines product design, UX/UI, and front-end development, which allows me to take ownership of complex problems and turn them into usable, scalable solutions.

I’ve worked on projects ranging from critical internal systems used by thousands of users to early-stage product concepts and MVPs, always focusing on clarity, reliability, and decision-making.

I’m particularly experienced in translating ambiguous requirements into structured flows aligning stakeholders, and delivering products that balance user needs, technical constraints, and business goals.
