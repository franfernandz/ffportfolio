ServiApp

Role: UI Design & Product Strategy

Focus: Trust-centered design, Scalable Systems, Visual Identity.

The Challenge

In emergency situations—like a gas leak or a broken lock—users face high stress and the risk of overpaying for unreliable service. The goal was to design a platform that connects homeowners with certified professionals quickly, safely, and transparently.

"How can we transform a stressful home emergency into a controlled, reliable experience?"

The Strategy: Designing for Trust

Based on the initial UX research, the key to user adoption was transparency in pricing, timing, and identity. I focused on translating these insights into a high-fidelity interface where the user always feels in control.

  • Professional Profiles: I prioritized the visibility of base pricing, real-time availability, and user ratings to eliminate the "hidden cost" anxiety.
  • The User Hub: I designed the Profile Screen as a central command center. It’s not just a settings page; it’s a "Control Hub" where users manage their entire lifecycle within the app, from active bookings to payment security.

Visual & Technical Decisions

Color Theory for Reliability: I implemented a palette of Deep Blues for authority and Serene Greens to evoke safety and efficiency. To create a modern, high-conversion environment, I used a Soft Pink as a tertiary accent for Call-to-Actions (CTAs), providing a sophisticated contrast that guides the eye.

Typography: I chose Urbanist, a modern sans-serif that balances a friendly, youthful feel with the formal structure required for a service platform.

The 8px Grid System: To ensure visual harmony and technical scalability, I built the entire UI on an 8px grid. This creates a "block-based" consistency that makes the interface feel solid, organized, and easy for developers to implement.

The AI-Enhanced Workflow

To optimize the design process, I leveraged Artificial Intelligence in three key areas:

  • Color Validation: Using AI to simulate accessibility and contrast ratios.
  • Copywriting: Crafting empathetic, clear messaging for success and error states.
  • Decision Support: Validating UI patterns against industry standards to ensure the "Happy Path" was friction-free.
